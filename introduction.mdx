## Quick Start Guide

You begin by signing up for a Paperguide account. Once logged in, navigate to the dashboard where you create your first space. Name it based on your project, such as "Product Roadmap 2024".

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user">
    Visit the Paperguide website and click the sign-up button. Enter your email and create a password. Verify your account via the confirmation email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="folder">
    From the dashboard, select "New Space". Provide a name and description. Choose visibility settings for your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages" icon="file-text">
    Inside the space, click "New Page". Title it and start writing. Use the editor for markdown or rich text.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Tip: Use keyboard shortcuts like `Ctrl + B` for bold text to speed up editing.
</Callout>
